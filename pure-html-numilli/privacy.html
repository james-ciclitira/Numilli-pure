We encode our cookies so that only we can interpret the information stored in them. The help section of most browsers or browser add-ons provides instructions on blocking, deleting, or disabling cookies.

You may encounter CoinSource cookies or pixel tags on websites that we do not control. For example, if you view a web page created by a third party or use an application developed by a third party, there may be a cookie or pixel tag placed by the web page or application. Likewise, these third parties may place cookies or pixel tags that are not subject to our control and the CoinSource Privacy Policy does not cover their use.
